Frequently Asked Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers to common queries about our GPS, API, and digital service solutions.

What services do you provide through your APIs?

We offer APIs for mobile recharges, DTH, electricity, AEPS, PAN card, DMT, and WhatsApp messaging—all through secure, scalable endpoints.

Can I track live vehicle locations using your GPS system?

Yes, our GPS platform provides real-time tracking, geo-fencing alerts, speed reports, and route history with detailed dashboards.

Is WhatsApp messaging included in every plan?

WhatsApp messaging API is available in the Business and Enterprise plans. It includes promotional, transactional, and support chat capabilities.

Do you provide white-label or reseller panels?

Yes, we offer a customizable white-label panel so you can manage your clients and agents under your brand with complete control.

How can I get started or test the services?

You can sign up for our Starter Plan (free) to test basic APIs and GPS features. For full access, upgrade to Business or Enterprise plans.
